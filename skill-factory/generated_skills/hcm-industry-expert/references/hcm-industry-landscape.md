# HCM Industry Landscape

## Market Structure and Size

### Market Segmentation

The HCM enterprise software market is segmented into several key categories:

1. **Talent Acquisition (ATS/Recruiting)**: Applicant tracking systems, candidate sourcing, recruitment marketing
2. **Core HR/HRIS**: Employee data management, payroll, benefits administration
3. **Talent Management**: Performance management, learning & development, succession planning
4. **Workforce Management**: Time & attendance, scheduling, labor optimization
5. **Employee Experience**: Engagement platforms, internal communications, employee self-service
6. **Analytics**: Workforce analytics, people analytics, predictive insights

### Market Size and Growth

- Global HCM software market: ~$25-30B (2024), growing at 8-12% CAGR
- Talent Acquisition segment: Largest at ~$8-10B
- Core HR segment: ~$6-8B
- Talent Management: ~$4-6B
- Workforce Management: ~$3-5B
- Employee Experience: Fastest growing at ~15-20% CAGR

## Market Segments

### By Company Size

- **Enterprise (5000+ employees)**: Suite players dominate (Workday, Oracle, SAP), complex implementations, high ACV ($500K-$2M+)
- **Mid-Market (500-5000 employees)**: Mix of suite and point solutions, ACV $50K-$500K
- **SMB (<500 employees)**: Point solutions and modern platforms, ACV $5K-$50K

### By Geography

- **North America**: Largest market, ~50% of global spend
- **EMEA**: Strong enterprise adoption, ~30% of market
- **APAC**: Fastest growing, ~15% of market
- **LATAM**: Emerging market, ~5% of market

### By Vertical

- **Healthcare**: Strong workforce management needs
- **Retail**: Workforce scheduling and management critical
- **Manufacturing**: Time & attendance, labor optimization
- **Financial Services**: Compliance-heavy, suite players
- **Technology**: Modern platforms, API-first preferences
- **Hospitality**: Workforce scheduling critical

## Integration Patterns and Ecosystem

### Integration Architecture

- **API-First Platforms**: Modern vendors (Greenhouse, Lever, Rippling) prioritize API integration
- **Marketplace Models**: Some vendors (SmartRecruiters, Workday) offer app marketplaces
- **iPaaS Integration**: Common platforms (Zapier, Workato, MuleSoft) for multi-vendor stacks
- **Single Sign-On (SSO)**: SAML, OAuth standards for authentication

### Ecosystem Dynamics

- **Suite vs. Point Solution**: Enterprises often use suite players, mid-market mixes suite and best-of-breed
- **Vendor Consolidation**: Trend toward consolidation (e.g., UKG merger, acquisitions)
- **Platform Plays**: Some vendors building platform ecosystems (Workday, Oracle)
- **Partner Networks**: Channel partners, system integrators, consultants

## Pricing Models and Revenue Structures

### Pricing Approaches

- **Per Employee Per Month (PEPM)**: Common for core HR, $5-15 per employee/month
- **Per Seat/User**: Common for talent acquisition, $50-200 per recruiter/month
- **Tiered Pricing**: Base + add-on modules, usage-based pricing
- **Enterprise Contracts**: Annual or multi-year, volume discounts

### Revenue Models

- **Pure SaaS**: Subscription revenue, recurring
- **Hybrid (SaaS + Services)**: Subscription + professional services
- **Managed Services**: Outsourced HR administration
- **BPO (Business Process Outsourcing)**: Full HR outsourcing
- **Platform + Marketplace**: Platform revenue + marketplace commissions

## Customer Acquisition Patterns

### Sales Motions

- **Enterprise**: Field sales, long sales cycles (6-12 months), high-touch
- **Mid-Market**: Mix of field and inside sales, 3-6 month cycles
- **SMB**: Inside sales, self-service, product-led growth, 1-3 month cycles

### Key Metrics by Segment

- **Enterprise**: Win rates 20-30%, ACV $500K-$2M, sales cycle 6-12 months
- **Mid-Market**: Win rates 40-50%, ACV $50K-$500K, sales cycle 3-6 months
- **SMB**: Win rates 50-60%, ACV $5K-$50K, sales cycle 1-3 months

### Proven GTM Playbooks

- **Workday**: Land-and-expand, start with financials, expand to HCM
- **Greenhouse**: Product-led growth, freemium model, strong developer relations
- **Rippling**: Cross-sell motion, HR + IT + Finance, viral growth
- **BambooHR**: SMB focus, inside sales, high volume
