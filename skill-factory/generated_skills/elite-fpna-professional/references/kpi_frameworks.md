# KPI Frameworks Guide
## Comprehensive Key Performance Indicator Frameworks

This guide provides KPI frameworks organized by category and industry, with definitions, calculations, targets, and monitoring frequency.

---

## Universal Financial KPIs

### Revenue Metrics

**Revenue Growth Rate**
- **Formula:** (Current Period Revenue - Prior Period Revenue) / Prior Period Revenue × 100
- **Target:** Varies by stage (Early: 50%+, Growth: 20-50%, Mature: 5-15%)
- **Frequency:** Monthly
- **Why it Matters:** Primary indicator of business momentum

**Revenue per Employee**
- **Formula:** Total Revenue / Number of Employees
- **Target:** $150K-$250K (services), $500K-$1M+ (software)
- **Frequency:** Quarterly
- **Why it Matters:** Organizational productivity indicator

**Revenue Retention Rate**
- **Formula:** (Revenue from Existing Customers / Prior Period Revenue from Those Customers) × 100
- **Target:** >95% (subscription businesses)
- **Frequency:** Monthly
- **Why it Matters:** Measures stickiness and churn

---

### Profitability Metrics

**Gross Margin %**
- **Formula:** (Revenue - COGS) / Revenue × 100
- **Target:** 70%+ (software), 40-60% (services), 20-40% (products)
- **Frequency:** Monthly
- **Why it Matters:** Indicates pricing power and unit economics

**Operating Margin %**
- **Formula:** Operating Income / Revenue × 100
- **Target:** 15-25% (healthy operating leverage)
- **Frequency:** Monthly
- **Why it Matters:** Core business profitability

**EBITDA Margin %**
- **Formula:** EBITDA / Revenue × 100
- **Target:** 20-30% (strong), 10-20% (moderate)
- **Frequency:** Monthly
- **Why it Matters:** Cash generation proxy

**Net Margin %**
- **Formula:** Net Income / Revenue × 100
- **Target:** 10-20% (healthy)
- **Frequency:** Monthly
- **Why it Matters:** Bottom-line profitability

---

### Cash Flow Metrics

**Operating Cash Flow Margin**
- **Formula:** Operating Cash Flow / Revenue × 100
- **Target:** 15-25%
- **Frequency:** Monthly
- **Why it Matters:** True cash generation ability

**Free Cash Flow**
- **Formula:** Operating Cash Flow - Capital Expenditures
- **Target:** Positive and growing
- **Frequency:** Monthly
- **Why it Matters:** Discretionary cash generation

**Cash Conversion Cycle**
- **Formula:** DSO + DIO - DPO
- **Target:** <45 days (excellent), 45-60 days (good)
- **Frequency:** Monthly
- **Why it Matters:** Working capital efficiency

**Burn Rate** (for growth companies)
- **Formula:** Monthly decrease in cash balance
- **Target:** Declining burn, path to profitability
- **Frequency:** Monthly
- **Why it Matters:** Survival metric

**Cash Runway**
- **Formula:** Cash Balance / Monthly Burn Rate
- **Target:** >12 months
- **Frequency:** Monthly
- **Why it Matters:** Time to profitability or funding need

---

### Efficiency Metrics

**Days Sales Outstanding (DSO)**
- **Formula:** (Accounts Receivable / Revenue) × 365
- **Target:** <45 days
- **Frequency:** Monthly
- **Why it Matters:** Collection efficiency

**Days Inventory Outstanding (DIO)**
- **Formula:** (Inventory / COGS) × 365
- **Target:** <60 days (varies by industry)
- **Frequency:** Monthly
- **Why it Matters:** Inventory efficiency

**Operating Expense Ratio**
- **Formula:** Operating Expenses / Revenue × 100
- **Target:** <35% (efficient)
- **Frequency:** Monthly
- **Why it Matters:** Cost discipline

---

## SaaS & Software KPIs

**Annual Recurring Revenue (ARR)**
- **Formula:** MRR × 12
- **Target:** Growing >30% annually
- **Frequency:** Monthly
- **Why it Matters:** Predictable revenue base

**Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR)**
- **Formula:** Sum of all recurring subscription revenue normalized to monthly
- **Target:** Consistent growth
- **Frequency:** Monthly
- **Why it Matters:** Subscription health

**Net Revenue Retention (NRR)**
- **Formula:** (Starting ARR + Expansion - Contraction - Churn) / Starting ARR × 100
- **Target:** >110% (excellent), >100% (good)
- **Frequency:** Monthly
- **Why it Matters:** Expansion minus churn

**Gross Revenue Retention (GRR)**
- **Formula:** (Starting ARR - Churn - Contraction) / Starting ARR × 100
- **Target:** >90% (excellent), >85% (good)
- **Frequency:** Monthly
- **Why it Matters:** Logo retention

**Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)**
- **Formula:** Sales & Marketing Expenses / New Customers Acquired
- **Target:** Varies, but must have good LTV/CAC ratio
- **Frequency:** Monthly
- **Why it Matters:** Efficiency of customer acquisition

**Customer Lifetime Value (LTV)**
- **Formula:** (ARPU × Gross Margin %) / Churn Rate
- **Target:** LTV/CAC ratio >3x
- **Frequency:** Quarterly
- **Why it Matters:** Total value of customer

**LTV/CAC Ratio**
- **Formula:** Customer Lifetime Value / Customer Acquisition Cost
- **Target:** >3x (healthy unit economics)
- **Frequency:** Quarterly
- **Why it Matters:** Unit economics sustainability

**CAC Payback Period**
- **Formula:** CAC / (Monthly Revenue per Customer × Gross Margin %)
- **Target:** <12 months (excellent), <18 months (acceptable)
- **Frequency:** Quarterly
- **Why it Matters:** How fast CAC investment pays back

**Rule of 40**
- **Formula:** Revenue Growth Rate % + Profit Margin %
- **Target:** >40% (healthy SaaS business)
- **Frequency:** Quarterly
- **Why it Matters:** Balance of growth and profitability

**Magic Number**
- **Formula:** Net New ARR / Prior Quarter Sales & Marketing Spend
- **Target:** >0.75 (efficient), >1.0 (excellent)
- **Frequency:** Quarterly
- **Why it Matters:** Sales & marketing efficiency

**Churn Rate**
- **Formula:** (Customers Lost / Starting Customers) × 100
- **Target:** <5% monthly (B2C), <2% monthly (B2B)
- **Frequency:** Monthly
- **Why it Matters:** Customer retention

**Average Revenue Per User (ARPU)**
- **Formula:** Total Revenue / Number of Customers
- **Target:** Growing over time
- **Frequency:** Monthly
- **Why it Matters:** Revenue per customer trends

## E-commerce & Retail KPIs

**Same-Store Sales Growth**
- **Formula:** (Current Period Sales - Prior Period Sales for Same Stores) / Prior Period × 100
- **Target:** >3% (healthy)
- **Frequency:** Monthly
- **Why it Matters:** Organic growth excluding new stores

**Conversion Rate**
- **Formula:** (Number of Purchases / Number of Visitors) × 100
- **Target:** 2-3% (online), 20-40% (physical)
- **Frequency:** Daily/Weekly
- **Why it Matters:** Sales effectiveness

**Average Order Value (AOV)**
- **Formula:** Total Revenue / Number of Orders
- **Target:** Growing over time
- **Frequency:** Weekly
- **Why it Matters:** Revenue per transaction

**Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)**
- **Formula:** Marketing Spend / New Customers Acquired
- **Target:** Payback within 6-12 months
- **Frequency:** Monthly
- **Why it Matters:** Marketing efficiency

**Inventory Turnover**
- **Formula:** COGS / Average Inventory
- **Target:** 6-12x annually (varies by category)
- **Frequency:** Monthly
- **Why it Matters:** Inventory efficiency

**Gross Merchandise Value (GMV)**
- **Formula:** Total value of merchandise sold
- **Target:** Growing (marketplace metric)
- **Frequency:** Daily
- **Why it Matters:** Marketplace volume

**Cart Abandonment Rate**
- **Formula:** (Carts Created - Purchases) / Carts Created × 100
- **Target:** <70%
- **Frequency:** Daily
- **Why it Matters:** Checkout optimization opportunity

**Repeat Purchase Rate**
- **Formula:** (Customers with >1 Purchase / Total Customers) × 100
- **Target:** >30%
- **Frequency:** Monthly
- **Why it Matters:** Customer loyalty

---

## Manufacturing KPIs

**Capacity Utilization**
- **Formula:** (Actual Output / Maximum Capacity) × 100
- **Target:** 75-85% (optimal)
- **Frequency:** Daily/Weekly
- **Why it Matters:** Asset efficiency

**Overall Equipment Effectiveness (OEE)**
- **Formula:** Availability % × Performance % × Quality %
- **Target:** >85% (world-class)
- **Frequency:** Daily
- **Why it Matters:** Manufacturing efficiency

**First Pass Yield**
- **Formula:** (Good Units / Total Units) × 100
- **Target:** >95%
- **Frequency:** Daily
- **Why it Matters:** Quality metric

**Manufacturing Cycle Time**
- **Formula:** Time from start to finish of production
- **Target:** Decreasing over time
- **Frequency:** Weekly
- **Why it Matters:** Efficiency and responsiveness

**Cost Per Unit**
- **Formula:** Total Production Cost / Units Produced
- **Target:** Decreasing with scale
- **Frequency:** Monthly
- **Why it Matters:** Profitability per unit

**Inventory Days on Hand**
- **Formula:** (Inventory / COGS) × 365
- **Target:** 30-60 days (varies)
- **Frequency:** Weekly
- **Why it Matters:** Working capital efficiency

**Scrap Rate**
- **Formula:** (Scrap / Total Production) × 100
- **Target:** <2%
- **Frequency:** Daily
- **Why it Matters:** Quality and cost

---

## Professional Services KPIs

**Utilization Rate**
- **Formula:** (Billable Hours / Total Available Hours) × 100
- **Target:** 70-80% (healthy)
- **Frequency:** Weekly
- **Why it Matters:** Resource efficiency

**Realization Rate**
- **Formula:** (Revenue Recognized / Standard Billing Rate) × 100
- **Target:** >90%
- **Frequency:** Monthly
- **Why it Matters:** Pricing and discounting

**Revenue Per Professional**
- **Formula:** Total Revenue / Number of Professionals
- **Target:** $200K-$400K
- **Frequency:** Quarterly
- **Why it Matters:** Productivity

**Client Retention Rate**
- **Formula:** (Clients Retained / Total Clients) × 100
- **Target:** >90%
- **Frequency:** Annually
- **Why it Matters:** Relationship quality

**Average Project Margin**
- **Formula:** (Project Revenue - Project Costs) / Project Revenue × 100
- **Target:** 30-50%
- **Frequency:** Per Project
- **Why it Matters:** Project profitability

**Billing Rate**
- **Formula:** Average rate charged per hour
- **Target:** Growing over time
- **Frequency:** Quarterly
- **Why it Matters:** Value pricing

## KPI Dashboard Design Principles

### 1. Keep It Simple
- 5-10 KPIs maximum for executive dashboard
- Focus on what matters most
- Use clear visualizations

### 2. Use Leading & Lagging Indicators
- **Leading:** Predict future performance (pipeline, bookings)
- **Lagging:** Measure past results (revenue, profit)
- Balance both types

### 3. Set Targets & Thresholds
- Define what "good" looks like
- Use traffic lights (green/yellow/red)
- Compare to prior periods and targets

### 4. Make It Actionable
- Link KPIs to actions
- Assign ownership
- Track initiatives to improve KPIs

### 5. Review Frequency
- **Daily:** Operational metrics
- **Weekly:** Tactical metrics
- **Monthly:** Financial metrics
- **Quarterly:** Strategic metrics

---

*Customize these KPI frameworks based on your specific business model, stage, and strategic priorities. Not all KPIs are relevant for all businesses.*
