ELITE FP&A PROFESSIONAL - INVOCATION PROMPTS
==============================================

This file contains example prompts to invoke and test the Elite FP&A Professional skill.

-----------------------------------------------------------
BASIC INVOCATION PROMPTS
-----------------------------------------------------------

1. COMPREHENSIVE FINANCIAL ANALYSIS
"Analyze these financial statements and provide a comprehensive CFO-level assessment. 
Identify key performance drivers, red flags, and strategic recommendations."

2. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY REQUEST
"Provide an executive summary of this company's financial health. What are the top 
3 strengths and top 3 concerns?"

3. PROFITABILITY DEEP DIVE
"Conduct a detailed profitability analysis. Where are we making money? Where are we 
losing money? What should we do about it?"

4. CASH FLOW ANALYSIS
"Analyze our cash flow and working capital. Why is our cash balance declining despite 
strong net income? What actions should we take?"

5. RED FLAG IDENTIFICATION
"Identify financial red flags in these statements. What concerns should management 
and the board be aware of?"

-----------------------------------------------------------
VARIANCE ANALYSIS PROMPTS
-----------------------------------------------------------

6. BUDGET VS ACTUAL
"Perform variance analysis for Q3 results vs. budget. What drove the performance miss? 
What corrective actions do you recommend?"

7. MARGIN ANALYSIS
"Analyze why our margins are declining. Is it pricing pressure, cost inflation, or 
unfavorable mix? What's the solution?"

8. REVENUE VARIANCE
"Our revenue came in 15% below budget this quarter. Decompose the variance into volume, 
price, and mix effects."

9. COST VARIANCE
"Operating expenses are $500K over budget. Analyze the drivers and recommend actions."

-----------------------------------------------------------
STRATEGIC ANALYSIS PROMPTS
-----------------------------------------------------------

10. CUSTOMER PROFITABILITY
"Analyze our customer profitability. Which customers are most valuable? Where should 
we focus our sales efforts?"

11. COST OPTIMIZATION
"Evaluate our cost structure and identify opportunities for margin expansion. Provide 
a detailed cost optimization roadmap."

12. WORKING CAPITAL EFFICIENCY
"Assess our working capital efficiency. How do we compare to industry peers? What 
improvements would you recommend?"

13. CAPITAL STRUCTURE
"Evaluate our capital structure. Do we have too much/too little debt? What's the 
optimal financing strategy?"

14. STRATEGIC INITIATIVE
"Perform strategic financial analysis for a potential product line expansion. What's 
the expected ROI? What are the risks?"

-----------------------------------------------------------
MODELING & FORECASTING PROMPTS
-----------------------------------------------------------

15. FINANCIAL MODEL
"Build a 3-year financial model with base/upside/downside scenarios. Include revenue 
drivers, cost structure, and cash flow projections."

16. CASH RUNWAY
"Forecast our cash needs for the next 18 months. Do we need to raise capital? If so, 
how much and when?"

17. SCENARIO ANALYSIS
"Model three scenarios: base case (20% growth), upside (35% growth), and downside 
(10% growth). What are the financial implications of each?"

18. SENSITIVITY ANALYSIS
"Perform sensitivity analysis on our financial model. What happens if gross margins 
compress by 200bp? Or if growth slows to 15%?"

-----------------------------------------------------------
INDUSTRY-SPECIFIC PROMPTS
-----------------------------------------------------------

19. SAAS METRICS (For SaaS companies)
"Calculate and analyze our SaaS metrics including ARR growth, NRR, GRR, CAC, LTV, 
CAC payback, Rule of 40, and Magic Number."

20. RETAIL METRICS (For Retail companies)
"Analyze our retail performance: same-store sales, inventory turnover, gross margin 
trends, and working capital efficiency."

21. UNIT ECONOMICS
"Analyze our unit economics. What's the true cost to acquire a customer vs. their 
lifetime value? Is our business model fundamentally profitable at scale?"

-----------------------------------------------------------
DASHBOARD & REPORTING PROMPTS
-----------------------------------------------------------

22. EXECUTIVE DASHBOARD
"Create an executive dashboard for the CEO and board. What are the 10 most important 
metrics to track?"

23. KPI FRAMEWORK
"Develop a comprehensive KPI framework for our business. What metrics should we track 
monthly/quarterly?"

24. BOARD REPORT
"Prepare a board-ready financial analysis report with executive summary, key findings, 
red flags, and recommendations."

-----------------------------------------------------------
COMPARATIVE ANALYSIS PROMPTS
-----------------------------------------------------------

25. PEER BENCHMARKING
"How do our financial metrics compare to industry benchmarks? Where are we 
overperforming or underperforming?"

26. TREND ANALYSIS
"Analyze financial trends over the past 3 years. What patterns emerge? Are trends 
improving or deteriorating?"

27. PERFORMANCE ATTRIBUTION
"Why did our EBITDA margin expand 300bp this year? Decompose the drivers (revenue 
growth, gross margin, operating leverage, etc.)."

-----------------------------------------------------------
SPECIALIZED ANALYSIS PROMPTS
-----------------------------------------------------------

28. QUALITY OF EARNINGS
"Assess the quality of earnings. How much of net income is converting to cash? Are 
there any accounting red flags?"

29. PRICING STRATEGY
"Analyze our pricing strategy. Are we leaving money on the table? Where can we raise 
prices without impacting volume?"

30. OPERATIONAL EFFICIENCY
"Evaluate our operational efficiency vs. competitors. Where are we underperforming? 
What drives the gap?"

31. DUE DILIGENCE
"Perform due diligence-level financial analysis on these statements for a potential 
acquisition or investment."

32. TURNAROUND ANALYSIS
"This company is underperforming. Perform a turnaround analysis: identify root causes 
and develop a financial improvement roadmap."

-----------------------------------------------------------
REAL-WORLD SCENARIO PROMPTS
-----------------------------------------------------------

33. BOARD MEETING PREP
"The board meeting is next week. Analyze our Q2 results and prepare talking points: 
highlights, concerns, and recommendations."

34. INVESTOR MEETING PREP
"We're meeting with investors next month. Prepare a comprehensive financial analysis 
highlighting our strengths and addressing potential concerns."

35. BUDGET REVIEW
"Review our annual budget submission. Is it realistic? What are the risks and 
opportunities?"

36. MONTHLY CLOSE
"It's month-end. Analyze the preliminary results vs. forecast. What are the key 
variances and what happened?"

-----------------------------------------------------------
ADVANCED ANALYSIS PROMPTS
-----------------------------------------------------------

37. COMPREHENSIVE RATIO ANALYSIS
"Calculate comprehensive financial ratios (liquidity, profitability, efficiency, 
leverage) and interpret the results."

38. DUPONT ANALYSIS
"Perform DuPont analysis on our ROE. What's driving our return: profitability, 
efficiency, or leverage?"

39. CASH FLOW WATERFALL
"Create a cash flow waterfall showing how we got from last quarter's ending cash 
to this quarter's ending cash."

40. MARGIN BRIDGE
"Build a margin bridge showing how we got from last year's gross margin of 42% 
to this year's 38%. What drove the compression?"

-----------------------------------------------------------
TESTING DIFFERENT BUSINESS SCENARIOS
-----------------------------------------------------------

41. HIGH GROWTH, UNPROFITABLE
"We're growing 80% YoY but burning $2M/month. Analyze our path to profitability 
and cash runway."

42. MATURE, PROFITABLE
"We're a mature business growing 8% with 25% EBITDA margins. Analyze our performance 
and recommend strategies to re-accelerate growth."

43. SEASONAL BUSINESS
"Our business is highly seasonal (70% of revenue in Q4). Analyze our working capital 
needs and cash flow patterns."

44. TURNAROUND SITUATION
"We've been declining for 3 years. Perform a diagnostic analysis and develop a 
turnaround roadmap."

45. RAPID SCALE-UP
"We're rapidly scaling from $10M to $50M revenue. What financial and operational 
challenges should we anticipate?"

-----------------------------------------------------------
USAGE NOTES
-----------------------------------------------------------

TIPS FOR EFFECTIVE USE:

1. PROVIDE CONTEXT: Include company description, industry, and any specific concerns

2. ATTACH DATA: Upload financial statements (Excel, PDF, or image) for best results

3. BE SPECIFIC: The more specific your question, the more targeted the analysis

4. REQUEST FORMAT: Specify if you want executive summary, detailed report, Excel model, etc.

5. MULTIPLE PERIODS: Provide multiple periods (quarters/years) for trend analysis

6. BENCHMARK REQUEST: Ask for industry benchmarking if relevant comp data exists

7. FOLLOW-UP: Ask follow-up questions to drill deeper into specific areas

EXAMPLES OF CONTEXT:

"We're a $25M ARR SaaS company growing 40% YoY, serving mid-market enterprises. 
[Then include your specific request]"

"We're a traditional manufacturing company with $100M revenue in the automotive 
parts sector. [Then include your specific request]"

"We're an early-stage startup with $2M in revenue, burning $300K/month. 
[Then include your specific request]"
