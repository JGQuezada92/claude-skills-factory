==============================================================================
USAGE NOTES
==============================================================================

When testing, verify:

1. COMPLETENESS
   - All phases present (Research, Priming, Comprehension)
   - 10-15 resources ranked
   - 10 quiz questions
   - 3-5 project prompts
   - Complete notes for all subtopics

2. FORMAT COMPLIANCE
   - Follows standardized template exactly
   - Proper markdown formatting
   - Audio-friendly language
   - Visual scanability

3. CONTENT QUALITY
   - Resources are real and high-quality
   - Quiz answers are correct
   - Examples are accurate
   - Definitions match expert consensus

4. MULTI-MODAL OPTIMIZATION
   - Conversational tone for audio
   - Clear structure for visual
   - Works for NotebookLM conversion

5. DOMAIN ADAPTATION
   - Teaching approach suits the subject
   - Technical subjects: precision, worked examples
   - Business: frameworks, case studies
   - Creative: masters, process
   - Science: mechanisms, multi-scale
